Overview

Where The Moss Grows Back is a narrative platformer about a small fox learning to let go of emotional weight. The game blends exploration and light platforming with a strong focus on atmosphere, visual storytelling and emotional pacing.

Accompanied by a glowing spirit, the fox moves through a world where nature reflects their inner state. Rather than focusing on difficulty, the experience is designed to feel gentle, reflective, and immersive.

Core Systems

This project is still a work in progress but I am hoping to achieve the following:

    • Expressive Movement: The fox’s movement changes subtly to reflect emotional state, affecting speed, weight, and jump feel.
    • Dual Form Mechanic(?): Players switch between physical fox and spirit form to solve traversal and platforming challenges.
    • Environmental Storytelling: Visuals, lighting, and sound evolve alongside the player’s emotional journey.
    • Low-Punishment Design: No harsh failure states - the focus is on accessibility and atmosphere.
